LIITLE INFORMATION ABOUT SITES

SWIGGY

Swiggy is the best-in-class food ordering app today. With interactive UI/UX and simple call to actions, Swiggy is gaining popularity among all age groups. The fast delivery, live order tracking, and the fact that there is no minimum order have made swiggy favourite among the working-class millennials. Also, it is appreciated that Swiggy is available in almost all cities in India. The offers and discounts the app offers, and the number of restaurants to choose from are some other factors making swiggy popular. Swiggy can be downloaded from the play store, and both iOS and Android versions are available for free. Different payment methods, including COD and online payment using debit and credit cards, are integrated for user’s convenience. The thought the makers have put into developing the app is much appreciated as it serves the purpose well.

UBEREATS

When the cab giant wanted to take a look into the food delivery service, UberEats was invented. This app allows you to search for food – category, location, or restaurant wise. Booking food is made easy through three easy steps. Browse, Order, and Track. UberEats also offers live order tracking and multiple payment options for the users. Contacting the delivery agent is also possible with this app. E-commerce apps have found a better stand-in India, so did the food delivery apps.

FOOD PANDA

Foodpanda is one of the pioneers of the online food delivery industry. The app has all your favourite food destinations and cuisines. Foodpanda is user-friendly and quick to order app. The steps to complete your order are convenient and super fast. Foodpanda is spread over a large part of India and acts as hunger saviours for many who are for some or the other reason searching for food online. Quick order delivery, exclusive deals, discounts, vouchers, and discounts throughout the year are other features of Foodpanda that attract the users

FAASOS

Faasos exists to answer the most asked question of the era. What’s for food? With a wide range of restaurants, Faasos helps its users to eat good and eat exciting. Faasos is available in over 15 cities in India, serving a variety of delicacies for foodies all over. Faasos has a premium version called bolt in which if the order is not delivered within 30 minutes, the order is free. The app also provides many offers and discounts and multiple payment options.

DELIVEROO

Considering the huge opportunities of the Indian online food delivery market, Deliveroo, the London based food ordering app, set foot in India. The food you ordered will reach you quickly, even in the busy hours and holidays. Efficient delivery is one of the best features of Deliveroo. Best restaurants available in your area can be searched, and your favourite cuisine will reach you within minutes.

GRUBHUB

Grubhub has the nation’s largest number of restaurants to choose from. Every store is included in the app for you to choose the best. The customer service is 24*7 for users’ convenience. Perks – the newest feature of Grubhub – helps you find all the deals running in the restaurants nearby. Some of the exclusive rewards and deals make Grubhub even more attractive to the users. The app is built using the latest UX design trends to impress the customers.

DOMINOS

Piping hot pizza at your doorstep. Domino’s app has a pizza for every occasion. Your app allows you to customize the order. The pizza delicacies are unimaginable and finger-licking good. That cheesy crust will melt your heart if you can have it in the comfort of your home. The app promises a 30 minute delivery time. Another attraction is that you can have a pizza even while on a train. Order your favourite pizza from the nearest domino’s outlet, and you will be received by the delivery agent at the next station. They’ve taken it to the next level.

SEAMLESS

Food ordering apps are competing to get better exposure among the hungry population of India. While approaching a mobile app development company to develop a food ordering app, the businesses ensure that they have a unique selling point which no one else offers. Seamless is recognized for its large list of restaurants and quick delivery. Item wise or restaurant wise searches, live order tracking, and multiple payment options are some other features that make Seamlessly recognizable.

PATAFO

Potafo is an online food delivery application based in Calicut, Kerala. They deliver delicious food to people from various restaurants in and around Calicut. The delivery is fast and neat. The customer service Potafo offers is appreciable and best. The app interface is simple and thus makes the food ordering hassle-free.

EAT FIT

When searching for mobile app ideas, the food delivery app will always top the list. Good food is something we all crave for. One of the serious issues today’s generation is dealing with is related to health. As the physical activities got reduced considerably, ailments started accompanying the Millenials. An app that helps you eat healthily is a boon. The app provides nutrition education and access to healthy foods. Customized meal plans can be searched before ordering your meal. Eat fit is an app that nourishes your healthy eating habits.
